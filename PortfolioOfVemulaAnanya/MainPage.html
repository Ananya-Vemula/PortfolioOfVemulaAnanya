MRI to CT Scan and Vice-versa

The objective of this project is to develop a GenAI-based framework for seamless conversion between MRI and CT scan images.


Tech Stack:

  • Python (Majorly used), TensorFlow, and TensorFlow Datasets

  • Considering Diffusion models and AG-GAN for finer details and better results
